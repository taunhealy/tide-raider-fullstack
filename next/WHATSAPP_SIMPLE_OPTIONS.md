# WhatsApp Providers - No Business Email or 2FA Required

Here are alternatives that don't require business email or 2FA:

## Option 1: Unipile (Recommended - Simplest)

**Pros:**
- ✅ QR code connection (like regular WhatsApp)
- ✅ No business verification needed
- ✅ No 2FA required
- ✅ Works with personal email
- ✅ No template approval needed
- ✅ Instant setup

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ Uses WhatsApp App connection (not official Business API)
- ⚠️ May have message limits

**Setup:** Connect via QR code, similar to WhatsApp Web

## Option 2: WaSenderAPI

**Pros:**
- ✅ Low cost ($6/month)
- ✅ Simple QR flow setup
- ✅ No Meta business verification
- ✅ Works with personal email
- ✅ Unlimited messages (according to their site)

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ Third-party service (not official Meta partner)

**Pricing:** Starting at $6/month

## Option 3: WhatsEra

**Pros:**
- ✅ No Facebook Business verification
- ✅ Limited access tier (250 unique customers/24h)
- ✅ Can upgrade automatically
- ✅ Works with personal email

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ Message limits initially (250/day)
- ⚠️ May need verification for higher tiers

## Option 4: WANotifier

**Pros:**
- ✅ Official Meta Tech Partner
- ✅ Direct WhatsApp Cloud API access
- ✅ Lower costs
- ✅ No intermediaries

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ May still have some verification requirements
